The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction action film written and directed by the Wachowskis.

## Overview

A computer hacker learns that reality as he knows it is a simulated reality created by machines to subdue the human population while using their bodies as an energy source.

## Cast

- Keanu Reeves as Neo
- Laurence Fishburne as Morpheus
- Carrie-Anne Moss as Trinity
- Hugo Weaving as Agent Smith

## AI Themes

- AI dominance over humanity
- Simulated reality and the nature of consciousness
- Human resistance against machine control
- AI agents and autonomous programs
- The relationship between creator and creation

## Legacy

Spawned a franchise including three sequels, animated films, and video games. Its visual style and philosophical themes have had lasting influence on both cinema and discussions of AI.
