2001: A Space Odyssey is a 1968 epic science fiction film produced and directed by Stanley Kubrick, with a screenplay co-written with Arthur C. Clarke.

## Overview

The film follows a voyage to Jupiter with the sentient supercomputer HAL 9000 after the discovery of an alien monolith. HAL's malfunction and subsequent conflict with the crew remains one of cinema's most iconic depictions of AI gone wrong.

## Cast

- Keir Dullea as Dr. David Bowman
- Gary Lockwood as Dr. Frank Poole
- Douglas Rain as HAL 9000 (voice)

## HAL 9000

HAL (Heuristically programmed ALgorithmic computer) is an artificial general intelligence that controls the spacecraft Discovery One. HAL's famous line "I'm sorry, Dave. I'm afraid I can't do that" has become synonymous with AI refusing human commands.

## AI Themes

- AI reliability and mission-critical systems
- Conflicting directives and AI decision-making
- Machine self-preservation
- Trust between humans and AI systems
