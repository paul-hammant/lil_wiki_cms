Humans is a science fiction television series that aired on Channel 4 and AMC from 2015 to 2018.

## Overview

Set in a parallel present where humanoid robots called "Synths" are commonplace household servants, the series follows several families and individuals as they navigate a world where the line between human and machine becomes increasingly blurred.

## Main Cast

- Gemma Chan as Mia/Anita
- Katherine Parkinson as Laura Hawkins
- Tom Goodman-Hill as Joe Hawkins
- Colin Morgan as Leo Elster
- Emily Berrington as Niska

## AI Themes

- Synthetic consciousness and rights
- Human-AI relationships and family dynamics
- Fear of technological unemployment
- What defines personhood
